Apollo 11

Adapted from Wikipedia:

Apollo 11 was the spaceflight that landed the first two humans on the Moon. Mission commander Neil Armstrong the Eagle on July 20, 1969. Amstrong became the first to step onto the lunar surface six hours after landing. Aldrin joined him about 20 minutes later. This landing was a culmination of President Kennedy's "Moon Speech" at Rice University in 1962.

Members of the Apollo 11 Mission

  • Neil Amstrong
  • Buzz Aldrin
  • Michael Collins
